Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Wordfive. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our word puzzle game.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from users. However, our website uses third-party services that may collect information:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • How often users visit the site
  • What pages they visit
  • What other sites they used prior to coming to our site
  • General location information (country/city level)

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. The information collected is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Local Storage

Our game may use your browser's local storage to save your game progress and preferences on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Payment or financial information
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Any user-generated content

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. The third-party services we use (Google Analytics and AdSense) are configured to comply with applicable privacy laws.

Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Delete cookies from your browser
  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only first-party cookies
  • Clear your browser's local storage

Note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of some features, such as saving your game progress.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us through our website.
